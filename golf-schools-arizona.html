Winter Golf Getaway

Rancho de los Caballeros Golf Schools

Wickenburg, AZ

Available Winter - Spring

Attend a 3-day/4-night golf school in sunny Arizona this winter. Golf school package includes lodging, meals, golf school instruction and golf course play lessons.

The home for Keith's winter golf school is Rancho de los Caballeros resort. For more than half a century the Ranch has been serving up western hospitality and style of the Spanish Caballeros.

Rated as one of America's Top 75 Resort Courses by Golf Digest, the Rancho de los Caballeros 18 hole championship course is packed with memorable holes amidst dramatic natural terrain and spectacular desert and mountain vistas.

Each day you will receive our award winning golf instruction on the Ranch's private lesson area before venturing out onto the golf course with your pro. The on-course instruction is designed to help you effectively transition from practice to play.

You'll learn how to manage your game during real playing conditions and develop a strategy to handle all the unique situations that you encounter during a typical round of golf. We'll show you how to think your way around the course, like a pro, so you can shave valuable strokes from your score. We'll discuss club and shot selection, unlevel lies, special shots and much more.

Class size is limited to just 6 golfers per session. And a small 3-to-1 student to instructor ratio ensures that you'll get plenty of personal attention.
