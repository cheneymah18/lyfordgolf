Junior Golf Clinics

Kids learn best through play. So all of our junior clinics include fun activities designed to accelerate learning and maximize each golfer's potential. We call this approach, teaching golf's FUN-damentals.

Ages 5-7

75% of the class time is devoted to speed development and learning Fundamental Movement Skills (FMS). One golf skill "theme-of-the-day" is introduced and the stations reinforce that golf fundamental. Juniors rotate between six stations, every five minutes.

Ages 8-10

This age group will start to transition from Fundamental Movement Skills to Fundamental Sports Skills (FSS) and introduce more complex golf-specific skills. One golf swing fundamental like wrist set, clubhead path or swing plane is stressed as juniors rotate between six stations, every ten minutes.

Ages 11-13

This session focuses on power development, flexibility, stamina and endurance. At this age, skill regression is common as bones are growing faster than muscle, so we focus on skill maintenance, not skill acquisition. This training circuit will address these unique age-appropriate training requirements.
