4-Day PGA Junior Golf Camps

Each player's game is evaluated to determine strengths and weaknesses, then a complete improvement plan is customized to his/her needs. Limiting class size to just 15 juniors per session and maintaining a 5 to 1 student to instructor ratio assures individualized attention.

Curriculum

The Golf Academy utilizes the same advance technology as Tour pros. Students will better understand their swing through the use of our 4-camera system and launch monitor technology. To make sure the practice time translates to lower scores, a significant amount of class time is spent on daily competitions and on-course play. Junior Golf Clinics

Juniors will learn golf fundamentals in a safe and fun environment. Sessions include individual instruction in a group setting so each student learns new skills based on his/her current golf level. Juniors rotate through a series of TPI (Titleist Performance Institute) exercise stations designed to teach youngsters motor skill development, core strength, balance and stability. Our primary goal is to create good all-around athletes that excel at golf.
