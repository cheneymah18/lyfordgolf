Keith Lyford

Keith has dedicated his life to finding better ways to help golfers improve their games and reach their golfing goals.

Keith’s experience running some of the largest and most popular golf schools in the country makes him uniquely qualified to teach a wide range of players, from brand new golfers to advanced competitive players.

Keith takes a simple, logical and straight-forward approach to helping his students and believes that you should see immediate improvement in both swing and ball flight results—even after just one lesson.

Keith is a three time PGA Teacher of the Year recipient. And Golf Magazine has selected Keith as one of the Top 100 Teachers in America for 22 consecutive years.

Keith has been the Director of Instruction at The Golf Academy at Old Greenwood in Truckee (Lake Tahoe) since 2009. The academy conducts junior camps and clinics, adult golf school and private lessons and club fitting each year from mid-May to mid-October.
